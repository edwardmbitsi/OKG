WHAT WE OFFER

Below are among the services we offer at OKG

BUILDING CONSTRUCTION

This is the process of us creating the physical structure, such as a home, office, or shopping center. It involves a range of activities, including design and planning, obtaining building permits, purchasing materials, and hiring contractors and workers to complete the construction.

PROJECT PLANNING

Project planning is a crucial aspect of construction, as it helps us ensure that a project is completed on time, within budget, and to the required quality standards. There are several key components to project planning in construction, we make sure they are followed.

HOUSE RENOVATION

We make changes to an existing home in order to improve its functionality, appearance, or value. Renovations can be as simple as painting a room or replacing old windows, or as complex as adding a new wing to the house or gutting the interior and starting from scratch.
